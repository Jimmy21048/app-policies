Privacy Policy

Last updated:03/02/2026

Eda is a financial management application designed to help users manage personal savings, group savings (such as Chamas or SACCOs), loans, and repayment reminders.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • User account identifiers
  • Group membership information

Financial Information

  • Savings contributions
  • Loan details and repayment schedules
  • Payment history and balances

We do not collect or store: card numbers, mobile money PINs, or bank passwords. The application is a pure planner, and doesn't handle any finances directly

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account creation and management
  • Savings and loan tracking
  • Payment reminders and notifications

3. Notifications

The App may send notifications for reminders and alerts. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted service providers or where required by law.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary. Users may request account and data deletion by contacting us.

7. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for users under 18 years of age.
