Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: 03/02/2026

This Account Deletion Policy explains how users of Eda can request deletion of their account and associated data from our financial management platform.

Important Notice: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Please read this policy carefully before proceeding with deletion.

1. How to Request Account Deletion

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  • Open the Eda application
  • Navigate to Profile → Account Settings
  • Scroll down to Account Actions
  • Tap "Delete Account"
  • Follow the confirmation prompts
  • Enter your passcode to confirm deletion

Method 2: Email Request

  • Send an email to: rubiajimmy3@gmail.com
  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include your registered email address and phone number
  • State your request clearly: "I want to delete my account"
  • We will respond within 7 business days

2. What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

Personal Information

  • Full name and contact details
  • Email address and phone number
  • User account credentials
  • Profile information and preferences

Financial Data

  • Personal savings accounts and balances
  • Loan records and payment history
  • Payment plans and schedules
  • Transaction history

Group Data (Where You Are Admin)

  • Chama/group information you created
  • Member lists and roles
  • Contribution records
  • Group financial transactions
Group Admin Responsibility: If you are an admin of any Chama or savings group, deleting your account will also delete the entire group and all associated member data. Please transfer admin rights to another member before deletion if you want to preserve the group.

3. Impact on Group Members

If You Are a Group Member:

  • Your contributions and participation records will be removed
  • Other members will see you as "Former Member" in historical records
  • Your financial contributions to the group will remain for group accounting

If You Are a Group Admin:

  • The entire group will be deleted unless you transfer admin rights first
  • All group members will lose access to the group
  • Group financial records will be permanently deleted
Before Deleting as Admin:
  1. Notify all group members about your intention to delete your account
  2. Transfer admin rights to a trusted member
  3. Ensure all financial obligations are settled
  4. Export any important group records if needed

4. Deletion Timeline

  • In-App Deletion: Immediate (within 24 hours)
  • Email Request: Processed within 7 business days
  • Data Removal: Complete within 30 days of confirmation

5. Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives before permanent deletion:

  • Account Deactivation: Temporarily disable your account
  • Data Export: Download your financial records first
  • Group Transfer: Transfer group ownership to continue group activities
  • Contact Support: Discuss concerns that might be resolved without deletion

6. After Deletion

  • You will receive email confirmation of successful deletion
  • You cannot recover your account or data after deletion
  • You can create a new account with the same email/phone if desired
  • Previous group memberships and contributions cannot be restored

7. Technical Considerations

  • Cache Clearing: Some data may remain in system caches temporarily

8. Your Rights

Under Kenyan data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request confirmation of data deletion
  • Receive a copy of data before deletion
  • File a complaint if deletion is not processed properly
  • Request information about what data is retained and why
