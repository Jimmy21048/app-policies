Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-09-26

Sovereign app respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • User Information: We collect your name and email address through a form during the first use of the app. This information is stored locally on your device and is not shared or transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Camera Access: We request access to your device’s camera to allow barcode scanning. Images or scans are processed locally and are not sent outside your device.
  • Advertising: Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may automatically collect certain information such as device identifiers, usage data, and location information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. For more details, please see Google’s Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • Your name and email are used only for creating and managing your local user account.
  • Your camera access is used only for barcode scanning.
  • Data collected by AdMob is used to serve ads.

Data Sharing

We do not share your name, email, or scanned data with any third party. AdMob may collect and share data in accordance with Google’s policies.

Data Storage & Security

All user-provided data (name, email, scans) is stored only on your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Your Rights

You may stop all data collection by uninstalling the app. Since we do not store your personal information on external servers, deletion is entirely under your control.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised effective date.
